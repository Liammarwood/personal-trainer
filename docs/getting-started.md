## Step 1: Access the Application

1. Navigate to the Personal Trainer web application
2. You'll see the sign-in screen with a Google sign-in button

{: .note }
Personal Trainer requires a Google account to track your workout history and save your progress.

## Step 2: Sign In

1. Click the **"Sign in with Google"** button
2. Select your Google account from the popup
3. Grant the necessary permissions
4. You'll be redirected to the main workout interface

![Sign In Screen](images/signin.png)

## Step 4: Select an Exercise

You have three ways to choose exercises:

### Option A: Upload a Workout Plan Image

1. Click the **"Upload Workout"** button
2. Select an image file (JPG, PNG) containing your workout plan
3. The AI will scan and extract exercises from the image
4. Review the detected exercises and confirm

{: .tip }
For best results, use clear, well-lit images of your workout plan with legible text.

### Option B: Choose from Exercise Library

1. Navigate to the **Workout Plan** section
2. Click **"Add Exercise"**
3. Browse the exercise library
4. Select your desired exercise
5. Set the number of sets and reps

### Option C: Manual Entry

1. Go to **Workout Plan**
2. Click **"Create Custom Plan"**
3. Enter exercise name, sets, and reps manually
4. Add multiple exercises to create a full routine

![Exercise Selection](images/exercise-selection.png)

## Step 5: Configure Settings

Before starting your workout, customize your experience:

1. Click the **Settings** icon (‚öôÔ∏è) in the top right
2. Adjust the following:

### Display Settings
- **Show Stats Overlay**: Toggle workout statistics on/off
- **Show Rep Quality**: Display form quality indicators
- **Advanced Mode**: Enable detailed metrics

### Audio Settings
- **Sound Enabled**: Toggle audio announcements on/off
  - Rep counts
  - Set completions
  - Workout milestones

### Processing Settings
- **Client-Side Processing**: Use local processing for privacy
  - Recommended for most users
  - Keeps your video data on your device

![Settings Menu](images/settings.png)

## Step 6: Start Your Workout

### For Webcam Mode:

1. Click **"Start Tracking"** on your selected exercise
2. Allow webcam access when prompted
3. Position yourself in frame
4. Begin your exercise
5. Watch as the AI counts your reps automatically

{: .warning }
Ensure good lighting and clear camera view for best tracking accuracy.

### For Video File Mode:

1. Click **"Upload Video"** in the Upload Interface
2. Select your workout video file (MP4)
3. The video will be processed automatically
4. View the analysis results when complete

![Workout in Progress](images/workout-active.png)

---

## Step 7: Monitor Your Progress

While working out, you'll see:

### Stats Overlay (if enabled)
- **Current Reps**: Number of reps in current set
- **Total Sets**: Sets completed vs. target
- **Duration**: Total workout time
- **Exercise Name**: Current exercise being tracked

### Audio Feedback (if enabled)
- Rep count announcements after each rep
- "Set X complete. Take a rest." after each set
- "Well done! Workout complete!" when finished

### Visual Indicators
- Green skeleton overlay showing detected pose
- Joint angle measurements
- Form quality indicators

![Stats Overlay](images/stats-overlay.png)

---

## Step 8: Complete Your Workout

1. Finish all sets for your exercise
2. Click **"Stop Tracking"** when done
3. Review your workout summary:
   - Total reps completed
   - Number of sets
   - Total duration
   - Average rep quality

4. Click **"Close"** to return to the main interface

![Workout Complete](images/workout-complete.png)

---

## Step 9: Track Multiple Exercises

To work through a complete workout plan:

1. Your workout plan shows all exercises
2. Completed exercises are marked with a checkmark ‚úì
3. Click on the next exercise to continue
4. Repeat the tracking process for each exercise

![Workout Plan Progress](images/workout-plan.png)

## Step 10: Sign Out

When you're done:

1. Click your profile icon in the top right
2. Select **"Sign Out"**
3. Your workout data is saved to your account

## Quick Tips

{: .highlight }
**Pro Tips for Best Results:**
- Use good lighting for better tracking accuracy
- Wear contrasting clothing from your background
- Keep your full body in frame
- Perform exercises at a steady pace
- Enable audio feedback for hands-free tracking
- Review your form using the joint angle displays

{: .note }
**Privacy Note:** When using client-side processing, all video analysis happens on your device. No video data is sent to servers.
