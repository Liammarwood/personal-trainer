## Quick Start

1. **Sign in** with your Google account
2. **Choose your input mode**: Webcam or Video File
3. **Select an exercise** from the library or upload a workout plan
4. **Start tracking** and let the AI count your reps
5. **Review your stats** and celebrate your progress!

## System Requirements

- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Webcam for live tracking (optional)
- Internet connection
- Google account for sign-in
